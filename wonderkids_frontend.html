About Us

A Place Where Every Child Shines

Wonder Kids Nursery & KG School, run by experienced educator Vaishali Jadhav, has been a cornerstone of early childhood education in Ahilyanagar since 2009. We believe the first years are the most important.

🌟

Child-Centred Learning

Activities designed around the natural curiosity and development stage of each child.

🎭

Holistic Development

Music, art, storytelling, yoga and outdoor play for full mind-body growth.

🤝

Parent Partnership

Regular updates, parent-teacher meets and open communication at all times.
